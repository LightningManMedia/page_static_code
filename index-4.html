Introducing PageConnect Groups!

A place where Page members can discuss issues, share content, learn from each other and make new connections.

As the Page community grows, it becomes more important to enable the intimate peer exchanges that make Page so uniquely valuable. That's why we're introducing PageConnect Groups, member-only affinity groups organized by topics of interest or industry sectors. Their purpose is to bring us together in new ways, enable us to share our knowledge and experiences and help make one another stronger, more effective leaders and communicators.

Throughout the year, each group will engage through webinars on a LinkedIn page and in-person meetings. All you need to do is join the LinkedIn groups for the Page Connect Groups you want to participate in and to take part when you're able and interested. Like any community, it thrives when we all participate, so we hope to see you in these groups and that you find value in engaging with your fellow members.
